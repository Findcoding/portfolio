// THE PHOTOGRAPHER

BIJENDAR PRASAD

Site Reliability Engineer & Fine Art Photographer based in Delhi, India.

“Photography is the art of capturing silent dialogues between light, architecture, and atmosphere before the moment dissipates.”

When I am not engineering high-availability cloud infrastructure and distributed ledger networks, I explore visual storytelling through fine art photography. My work emphasizes symmetry, geometrical minimalism, high-contrast natural lighting, and the quiet intimacy of human environments.
